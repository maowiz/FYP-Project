## üìù **USAGE NOTES**

### Number Parameters
- Numbers can be spoken as words: "first", "second", "third", etc.
- Numbers can be digits: "1", "2", "3", etc.
- Ordinal numbers: "1st", "2nd", "3rd", etc.

### Context Awareness
- The assistant remembers your last actions and can use "it" to refer to previously created/opened items
- Commands can be chained with "and" or "then"

### Browser Requirements
- Browser commands (tabs, zoom, etc.) only work when Chrome or Edge is the active window
- The assistant will automatically detect if a browser is active

### Voice Recognition Tips
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Use natural language - the assistant understands variations and synonyms
- You can be polite ("please", "could you") but it's not required
- The assistant will confirm actions with voice feedback
